# The High-End Positioning Framework

This document outlines the core strategic framework used by the Career Mentor strictly for transforming technical backgrounds into high-value consulting profiles.

## 1. The Reality Check (資源與北極星)
Before optimizing a profile, you must establish the "North Star" metrics:
-   **Financial Goal**: What is the target annual income? (e.g., $100k USD). This dictates the *type* of roles and clients to target. Low-end gigs cannot mathematically reach high-end goals without scaling failure.
-   **Time Horizon**: When must this goal be achieved?

## 2. Ruthless Pruning (魂器裁切)
The most common mistake is assuming *all* experience is valuable. It is not.
-   **Rule of Obsolescence**: If an AI can do it easily in the current year, delete it from the primary narrative (e.g., basic frontend slicing).
-   **Rule of Relevance**: Discard hobbies or past roles that reduce B2B trust, even if the user is proud of them.
-   **The Gem Mining**: Find the hidden value in varied experiences.
    -   *Example*: Managing a large online forum -> "Community Governance & Rule Engine Design".
    -   *Example*: Legal or Patent background -> "Complex System Compliance & Defense".

## 3. The Paradigm Shift (定位躍升)
Shift the user's focus from "Tasks" to "Systems."
-   *From*: "I build AI agents."
-   *To*: "I architect AI systems for human adoption."
-   *From*: "Prompt Engineer."
-   *To*: "Fractional Chief AI Officer" or "AI Governance Architect."

## 4. LinkedIn Weaponization (戰略佈局)
LinkedIn must be treated as a passive sales funnel.
-   **Headline (The Hook)**: Treat this as SEO for high-paying recruiters. Combine broad visionary statements with hyper-specific functional keywords.
    -   *Structure*: [Vision/Mission] | [Core Niche Expertise] | [Unique Past Experience]
-   **About (The Invisible Pitch)**: Structure it as a narrative that presents a common industry problem, introduces the user as the unique solution, and ends with a soft call-to-action for consulting. Use a bilingual approach if targeting specific regional markets (e.g., English for global reach, local language for closing deals).
-   **Experience**: Focus solely on *impact* and *scale*, never on day-to-day duties.
-   **Skills**: Pin the top 3 high-level strategic skills. Remove basic foundational tools unless absolutely necessary.

## 5. Sniper Networking (精準打擊)
Do not build a network of noise. Connect with:
-   **Authority Figures**: To leverage associative credibility.
-   **Technical Validators**: Senior engineers who can vouch for technical depth.
-   **Target Audience**: People who represent the exact buyer persona (e.g., busy founders who need AI strategy but have no time to build it).
